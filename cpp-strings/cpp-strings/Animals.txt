monkey:a wild animal that can climb trees fast and easily
leopard:A large spotted animal in the cat family.
duck:An animal can swim and says "Quack! Quack!".
crocodile:An animal can snap and has a long mouth.
peacock:The animal with hundreds of "eyes"
ant:a small insect that lives in large groups
elephant:a very large grey animal with four legs, two tusks
rabbit:a small animal with long ears and soft fur
deer:a large wild animal that can run very fast, eats grass, and has horns
cat:a small animal with four legs that people often keep as a pet.
goat:an animal that has horns on top of its head and long hair under its chin, and can climb steep hills and rocks.
