Line:the set of all points collinear to two points
Segment:two end points and all the points in between them
Plane:a flat figure with no thickness that extends indefinitely in all directions
Ray:is a line with a single endpoint (or point of origin) that extends infinitely in one direction.
Acute:angle whose measure is less than 90
Vertex:point in common of two rays that form an angle
Midpoint:the middle point of the line segment
Obtuse:an angle whose measure is greater than 90 and less than 180
